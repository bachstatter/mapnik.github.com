Regular expressions are very useful when working with text.We already had 'match' operator which can be used in filterexpressions. In Mapnik2 wenow also have 

<mapnik-expression>.replace(<regex>,<pattern>)
Here is a small example :
>>> from mapnik import *>>> f = Feature(123)>>> f["name"] = 'Mapnik'>>> expr = Expression("[name].replace('(\\B)|( )','$1 ')")>>> expr.evaluate(f)'M a p n i k'
